Use cases

One tool, every way
you run a release.

From a tight managed roster to a thousand-clip open pool, Crescendo tracks the posts, overlays the streams, and hands clients a link. Here is how teams put it to work.

01 · UGC campaigns

Run creator rosters without the spreadsheet.

Brief a roster, drop in the posts, and Crescendo takes it from there. We auto-detect each creator's post, refresh views, likes, comments, and shares every day, and score every post for promotion-relevance, so you know which content is actually about the track and which is just noise. See per-creator and per-post performance, then kill the duds and scale the winners while the campaign is still live.

  • Auto-detect posts across TikTok, Instagram, YouTube, and X
  • Daily refresh, plus a 30-day tail after the campaign wraps
  • Per-creator and per-post breakdowns with relevance scoring
02 · Clipping

Track a thousand clips, find the ten that mattered.

Open-pool and clipping campaigns generate volume fast. Paste a batch of links or drop a CSV, and Crescendo scrapes the entire pool, ranks every clip by views and promotion-relevance, and surfaces the breakouts. Flip between an approved-creators view and the full open pool, then pay on performance and pour budget into what is already working.

  • Bulk import by paste or CSV, no manual entry
  • Approved-versus-open-pool toggle for managed or mass posting
  • Rank by views and relevance so you pay for results, not noise
03 · Streaming lift

Prove the social push moved the streams.

This is the whole point. Crescendo overlays Spotify and SoundCloud on the same timeline as your UGC, so you watch the posts go live, the views climb, and the streams follow a beat later, all on one chart. When a clip pops and saves spike two days later, the dates line up in front of you. We show correlation, not proof of causation, and we label it that way, because honest beats hand-wavy.

  • UGC views and DSP streams on a single, shared timeline
  • Spotify and SoundCloud overlay, refreshed daily
  • Spikes and lead-lag called out, so the story tells itself
04 · Client reporting

Send a link, not a deck.

Stop rebuilding the same recap graph every Friday. Crescendo gives you white-label, read-only share links, so a client or label sees a clean, always-live dashboard of the work without ever touching your financials or your other accounts. Swap the logo, accent color, and footer per client, and let the numbers speak while you get your week back.

  • White-label share links, your logo, accent, and footer
  • Read-only by design, financials and other clients stay private
  • Always live, so there is no more Friday-night export ritual

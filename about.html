About

Built by the team that's
run 4,000+ campaigns.

Crescendo is the proof layer we wished we had. It comes from Artist Influence, the music marketing company that's spent years putting posts and streams in front of artists, and got tired of stitching the story together by hand.

Artist Influence started in 2019, out of an NYU dorm, with a simple frustration: music marketing was loud on promises and quiet on proof. Our founder, Jared Rapoza, had just come from running Electronic & Pop at Audiomack, and kept seeing the same gap, campaigns moved real numbers, but nobody could show it cleanly.

So we started running campaigns and measuring everything. Spotify playlisting, SoundCloud reposts, Instagram seeding, TikTok UGC, YouTube and Meta. Over 4,000 campaigns later, we'd grown from an agency into a software company, but one thing never got easier: connecting the social push to the streaming response. Every recap meant exporting posts from four platforms, pasting in Spotify numbers, and building the same line graph by hand, again.

That spreadsheet became Crescendo. We took the thing we rebuilt for every client and made it live: every post and every stream on one timeline, updated daily, so you can finally see the correlation instead of describing it. It is the tool we needed as marketers, which is exactly who we built it for.

What we believe
Four principles, built into the product.

Platform-native

TikTok, Instagram, YouTube, X, Spotify, and SoundCloud each behave differently. We track each the way it actually works, instead of forcing one generic metric.

Systems-first

One connected system beats five disconnected vendors and a folder of CSVs. Crescendo replaces the patchwork with a single source of truth.

Outcome-driven

Views are nice; streams pay rent. We tie social activity to the outcomes a release actually cares about, saves, plays, and streaming lift.

Transparent

Clients should see what happened, not just hear about it. White-label share links give them a clean, honest read on the work, correlation, not causation, and we label it that way.

Who it's for
Labels, managers, and the teams behind the breakouts.

We've run campaigns alongside major labels and independent artists across dance, electronic, and internet-native music. Crescendo is built for the people doing that work every day, marketers who need to prove the push moved the numbers, not just feel like it did. And when a release needs the boost and not just the proof, Artist Influence can run the campaign itself.

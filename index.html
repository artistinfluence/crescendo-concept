Two things no tracker does

Everything else is a glorified spreadsheet and line graph. These are two reasons why teams switch.

Honestly, we just got fed up. We run these campaigns for a living, and every tool out there was a glorified spreadsheet bolted to a line chart. So we built the two things we always wished existed. The stupid part is that nobody else had.

Self-learning

Rosters that get smarter every campaign

The Creator Database remembers how every creator and page performed in past campaigns: median views, engagement, cost per thousand views, and sample size. The Campaign Builder turns a budget plus a niche into a ranked, auto-selected roster with projected views and a per-creator FITS score. Every campaign you run feeds the database, so the next recommendation is sharper than the last.

Streaming correlation

See if the push moved the streams

For music clients, Crescendo overlays Spotify streams and SoundCloud plays on the same timeline as your UGC. You watch the posts go live, the views climb, and the streams follow a beat later, all on one chart, so you can show a client the social activity and the streaming response in a single view. Correlation, not proof of causation, and we label it that way.

Everything else

The tracking you already expect

Done well, in the dark, crimson aesthetic your clients will not mistake for anyone else.

Mass import, then scrape

Paste a batch of TikTok, Instagram, YouTube Shorts and X links or drop a CSV. Crescendo auto-detects the URL column and scrapes views, likes, comments, and shares on a schedule.

Client share links

Send a polished, read-only dashboard at a public link. Financials never leave your workspace, and white-label branding swaps the logo, accent, and footer per client.

Two campaign types

UGC for managed creator rosters that feed your database, Clipping for open-pool mass posting with an approved-versus-all client toggle. Two types, that is it.

Promotion relevance

Set what each campaign promotes (song, artist, lyrics) and Crescendo reads the comments to score the share that is actually on topic, with a theme breakdown.

Per-post analytics

Every post tracked individually: views, engagement rate, relevance, and thumbnails, sortable and rolled into live campaign totals.

Per-campaign analytics

One unified graph overlays cumulative views, engagements, official-sound creates, and streaming on a single timeline with a metric toggle.

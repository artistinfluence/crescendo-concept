Privacy Policy

Last updated June 23, 2026

This Privacy Policy explains what information Crescendo ("Crescendo," "we," "us") collects, how we use it, and the choices you have. Crescendo is a UGC and streaming analytics tool for music marketing teams. By using the Service you agree to the practices described here.

1. Information we collect

  • Account information. Name, work email, workspace name, and team members you invite.
  • Billing information. Plan, subscription status, and payment details, which are handled by our payment processor. We do not store full card numbers.
  • Campaign inputs. The post links, handles, tracks, and campaign settings you add to the Service.
  • Tracked metrics. Publicly available social engagement (views, likes, comments, shares) and the streaming figures you connect from sources such as Spotify and SoundCloud.
  • Usage and device data. Log data, approximate location, browser and device type, and product interactions, used to keep the Service secure and reliable.

2. How we use information

We use the information above to provide and operate the Service, build your dashboards and reports, calculate correlation between social activity and streaming, process payments, communicate with you, prevent abuse and fraud, meet legal obligations, and improve our features.

3. Data we access on your behalf

To run a campaign, Crescendo continuously collects publicly available posts and the streaming metrics you connect, and aggregates them into your workspace. Delivering, securing, maintaining, and improving the Service necessarily means that Crescendo and our authorized systems may access, view, and process this data, including the campaign content, engagement metrics, and streaming figures associated with your account, for as long as the related campaigns are active.

We treat that access as a responsibility rather than a license. We do not sell it, mine it for purposes unrelated to your account, or otherwise abuse it, and we limit internal access to the team members and systems that genuinely need it to operate, support, and secure the product. Where we rely on this access for analytics or product improvement, we work with aggregated or de-identified data wherever it is practical to do so.

4. How we share information

We do not sell your personal information. We share it only with: service providers and subprocessors that run our infrastructure, scraping, payments, and email under contract; parties to a merger or acquisition, with notice; and authorities where required by law or to protect rights, safety, and the integrity of the Service.

5. Data retention

We retain account and campaign data for as long as your account is active. Active campaigns refresh daily and keep tracking for up to 30 days after they wrap, after which they auto-archive. You can request deletion of your workspace data at any time, subject to legal and accounting requirements.

6. Security

We use industry-standard safeguards, including encryption in transit, access controls, and least-privilege practices, to protect your information. No method of transmission or storage is perfectly secure, but we work to protect your data and to notify you of material incidents as required by law.

7. Your rights and choices

Depending on where you live, you may have the right to access, correct, export, or delete your personal information, and to object to or restrict certain processing. To exercise these rights, contact us at tribe@artistinfluence.com. You can also manage team access and disconnect data sources from inside your workspace.

8. International users

Crescendo is operated from the United States. If you use the Service from elsewhere, you understand your information may be processed in the United States and other countries where we or our subprocessors operate.

9. Children

The Service is built for businesses and is not directed to children under 16. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children.

10. Changes to this policy

We may update this policy from time to time. When we make material changes, we will update the date above and, where appropriate, notify you in the product or by email.

Questions about this policy? Email tribe@artistinfluence.com. See also our Terms of Service.
